What is a Revenue OS?

In simple terms — an AI platform that runs your entire revenue engine, not just one part of it.

Much like an OS enables apps on your phone, Syntra uses state-of-the-art AI agents to run your Sales & Marketing workflows continuously. Syntra consists of 3 foundational building blocks:

Experiences
Deliver 1:1 personalized engagement across any channel — email, LinkedIn, web, or mobile — with pixel-perfect brand compliance at scale.
Intelligence
Deploy AI agents with state-of-the-art models and compose them into workflows with human-in-the-loop automation at every critical decision point.
Data
Integrate structured and unstructured data into a tiered revenue data lake with strict access controls and governance policies built in.

Nova, your AI assistant powered by Syntra, is available in Slack, Google Slides, Sheets and Email. Nova leverages a team of AI agents and workflows to assist your revenue team with every task.

What are the main use cases for Syntra?

  • Agentic Outbound & ABM

What does the name "Syntra" mean?
Syntra combines "synthesis" and "traction" — reflecting our mission to synthesize your revenue data, workflows and team into one unified system that drives real results.
Why do you call it an operating system?
Just like your phone's OS coordinates apps, memory and hardware — Syntra coordinates your agents, data and workflows. Point AI tools are just apps. Syntra is the OS beneath them.
What is your pricing model?
Outcome-based pricing tied to pipeline generated and deals closed — not seat licenses. Contact us to get a custom quote tailored to your team size and use case.
How are you different from other AI GTM vendors?
Most AI GTM tools automate one thing — a single email, a single channel. Syntra coordinates agents, data and workflows across your entire revenue motion. It's the difference between a single app and an operating system.
